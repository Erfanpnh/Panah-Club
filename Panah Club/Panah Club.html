Introduction


Welcome to Panah Club, a dynamic hub for aspiring young artists worldwide who are self-taught and passionate about their craft. At Panah Club, we're dedicated to fostering a vibrant community where talent knows no boundaries. Whether you're a self-taught painter, photographer, musician, writer, or any other form of creative expression, this is your platform to connect, collaborate, and flourish. Join us at Panah Club and be part of a global movement where your artistry is celebrated, your voice is heard, and your talent is given the spotlight it deserves. Let your creativity soar with Panah Club as we embark on this inspiring journey together.

Who is Panahi?


Meet Panahi, a self-taught artist recognized as Bllannd across Instagram and various social media platforms. His creation, Bllanndie, a character characterized by three simple lines, has garnered widespread acclaim for its unique and captivating aesthetic. In addition to his artistic endeavors, Panahi collaborates with rappers, contributing his talents to the vibrant realms of rap and Hip-Hop, striving to establish a notable presence within the industry. Recently, Panahi has embarked on an ambitious venture: the creation of "Panah Club," a public hub aimed at uniting self-taught artists from around the globe. This innovative initiative seeks to facilitate invaluable connections among artists, offering a platform for networking and collaboration that transcends traditional boundaries. Panah Club aims to streamline the process of forging meaningful relationships within the artistic community, fostering a dynamic environment conducive to creative growth and innovation.
